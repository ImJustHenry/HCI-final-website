# Bouncr

> **CS 321 - Human Computer Interaction** | Final Project

**Navigate your world, Seamlessly.**

Bouncr is a transportation planning app designed to help users easily find, compare, and plan their travel options. Whether it's public transit, rideshare services like Uber and Lyft, or other alternatives, Bouncr brings all your options together in one place — making navigating your world effortless and affordable.

---

## The Problem

Rising costs of car loans, insurance, and maintenance have placed a significant financial burden on many people. As owning a car becomes increasingly expensive, more individuals are seeking alternatives. Public transportation offers a cost-effective option, but access to it isn't always convenient or straightforward for everyone.

## Our Solution

Bouncr aggregates transportation options and lets users compare prices, routes, and travel times — all in a single seamless interface. Whether you prefer buses, trains, or rideshare, Bouncr helps you make the smartest choice for your journey.
